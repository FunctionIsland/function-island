1. Buy Resources

You will need to buy Resources with TRX to start your island - convert them into Island Power, and you'll start generating more Island Resources at a faster rate!


2. Convert Resources

Convert your generated Island Resources into more Island Power, and generate more resources faster! Converting frequently compounds your earnings!


3. Sell Resources

When you're generating lots of resources, you can sell them for TRX! You retain your power, but your generation starts again - Be sure to sell carefully!
